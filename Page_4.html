About "Bibingka";

Bibingka is a food in the Philippines and is one of the many varieties of rice cakes in Filipino cuisine. Its taste is usually slightly sweet, with a little bit of salty undertone for the contrast. Depending on the versions, this rice cake's texture can range from being spongy to being sticky..(back to Home)
